Data Deletion Request

At Triumpeer, we respect your right to privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your personal data at any time.

In-App Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data directly in any Triumpeer application by using the Delete Account option in the settings or help menu.

Request by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by sending an email to support@triumpeer.com from the email address linked to your account. Our team will process your request within 48 hours.

Data Retention Exceptions

Please note that we may retain certain information where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.
